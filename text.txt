Project Goals:

Develop an interactive online forum similar to Evangadi, focused on community engagement and discussion.

My Solution:

Designed a user-friendly interface with React and Vite, built a scalable back-end with Node.js and MySQL, implemented user authentication, real-time updates, and discussion features.

Impact:

Enhanced community interaction, improved user engagement, provided a reliable and scalable platform for discussions, and supported the growth of an active online community.
