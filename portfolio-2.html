Netflix Clone: Replicating the Ultimate Streaming Experience

Project Goals:

To recreate the immersive streaming experience of Netflix, offering a user-friendly interface, personalized recommendations, and seamless video playback.

My Solution:

Leveraged the YouTube API to fetch and display videos dynamically, implemented user authentication and session management.

Impact:

Achieved seamless video playback without the need for a database, provided a wide range of content through YouTube integration, ensured user security resulting in a successful Netflix-like streaming experience.
